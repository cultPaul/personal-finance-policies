Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

App

Personal Finance is a private personal finance tool used only by the app owner to view and analyse their own linked bank account information.

Data Accessed

When authorised by the account holder, the app may access account details, balances, and transaction history from linked bank accounts.

Purpose

The app uses bank account data only to support personal budgeting, transaction review, and private financial analysis for the app owner.

Storage

Data is stored only in private local systems or private repositories controlled by the app owner. The app is not offered as a public service.

Sharing

Bank account data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing. Data is shared only where required to operate the authorised banking connection or where required by law.

Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only while useful for the app owner's personal finance records. The app owner may delete locally stored data at any time.

Access Revocation

Bank access can be revoked through the relevant bank, account provider, or Enable Banking authorisation controls.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact email registered with the Personal Finance application.
